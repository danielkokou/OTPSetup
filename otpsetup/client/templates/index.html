Get Started with OpenTripPlanner the Quick and Easy Way

OTP Deployer is an online service for launching deployments of OpenTripPlanner, the leading open source platform for multimodal trip itinerary planning and analysis.

Simply submit your transit data as one or more GTFS feed(s), and a live OTP instance will be built for you. You'll be notified by email once complete, and your demo server will be online for a week — at no cost!

If you want to keep going after that, you can do so on a monthly basis. Or, simply download the graph and host it elsewhere.

To get started, create a free account. Once logged in, you may create an instance request.
